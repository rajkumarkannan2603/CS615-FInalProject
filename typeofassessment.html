Type of Assessments

Leadership Assessment

The success of Agile development initiatives will be either enhanced or undermined by your organization's leaders. The Apex leadership assessment measures quantitatively the skills and capacities of your leadership team. According to the Agile Manifesto, the following principles are of most importance to leaders. First, creating value: does leadership know that the value creation instead of feature creation? Second, encouraging change: does leadership not only welcome change, and not only produce change, but indeed reward change? And third, deliver frequently: does leadership drive the organization to processes favorable to continued development and provision? Apex's leadership assessment thoroughly examines leadership ability in your organization, and it generates suggestions to place leadership on the road to agility.

Organisation Assessment

As useful as Agile is to your teams working on software, the same principles hold true to your organization. The Apex Organization Assessment will measure your current skills as an Agile company. We will identify areas in which you are going in the right direction and those where you are practicing things that are bad. Such as a well-tested and tried family recipe, we've extracted our experience that spans over four decades of serving the software community to make a comprehensive assessment of software development practices. Among others, we measure the following key skills: value: do every your product releases produce recognizable and beneficial to the customer; willingness to change: are your organizations open and prompts for changing requirements, both early and late in the product cycle; and frequent deliveries via a continuous improvement and delivery pipeline.

Portfolio Assessment

Rarely does a company produce a single product or feature that is divorced from other cooperating products. Under a structure of several and concurrent development efforts, a siloed product development model is counterproductive. Do your product teams operate independently or is there a cooperative spirit aligning your goals? Is progress gated by milestones and measured by the amount of work completed? Most of the same techniques you accrue to iterative software development as well as apply to portfolio management. Our portfolio evaluation identifies where you are on an Agile continuum and more importantly, what practices can you make that brings value, open-mindedness to change, and improved delivery to your product portfolio.

Program Assessment

The Apex program evaluation measures how well your development strategy for programs matches up with your product portfolio. In an organization with several simultaneous development streams, cooperation and good intentions are not enough to bring about desired outcomes. Apex measures the program stakeholders' participation and considers strategies for predictability and problem resolution. Apex views program planning - is it in line with the rest of the programs in your enterprise portfolio? The Apex program assessment measures the probability of your program to exist together in sync within the portfolio and add value while being open to change.

Team Assessment

There are two significant points to consider. First, there is no ideal team situation. Second, it is so much work to create an atmosphere in which teams can thrive. The Apex Team Assessment looks at the makeup and dynamics of an individual agile team. We ascertain to what degree has the team gelled or what are the barriers to that happening. We examine the team dynamics: what level of trust is there between team members? Is there a team culture, and if so, is the culture beneficial for, or damaging to, team success? Does the team have the ability to provide excellent products? Apex's decades of experience working in Agile organizations enables us to acknowledge and evaluate those elements of teamwork that promote the creation of value, being receptive to evolve, and facilitate continuous improvement and delivery.

Individual Assessment

Apex in-house experts are not merely Agile development methodology specialists, but we possess gained that experience over years of working in different software industries. We understand individuals like your key contributors because we have also been key contributors. Drawing from in-depth domain expertise, Apex can assess the competency of the key individuals in your organization. The core Agile principles of value delivery, adaptability to change, and optimizing delivery is representative of trans-change skills and behavior. Our tests initially measure where your key contributors are in an Agile continuum. Additionally, the assessment provides concrete recommendations that can assist them in excelling.
